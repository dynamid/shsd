How to use SHSD ?

  • Make SHSD your default homepage
  • Watch from where your account has been used
  • Based on habits, you should progressively see that everything is quite static
  • If some points are in a strange area (a foreign country for instance) or if you do not recognize the name of one of your Internet Service Providers, contact your hoster
Please keep in mind that geolocation is not exact and sometimes even clearly wrong. Cellular networks (3G/4G) usually provide very poor location precision (country only) while ADSL/Cable/Fiber networks provide better geolocation.
